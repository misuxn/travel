Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

For international travel, you'll need:

  • A valid passport (with at least 6 months validity beyond your return date)
  • Visas (depending on your destination)
  • Proof of vaccinations (for certain countries)
  • Travel insurance documents
  • Flight itineraries and hotel confirmations

We provide a personalized checklist for each destination when you book with us.

We recommend booking as early as possible for the best prices and availability:

  • International trips: 6-12 months in advance
  • Domestic trips: 3-6 months in advance
  • Last-minute deals: Sometimes available 2-4 weeks before departure

Popular destinations and peak seasons (summer, holidays) book up fastest.

Our cancellation policy varies by package:

  • Standard packages: Full refund if cancelled 60+ days before departure (minus non-refundable deposits)
  • Premium packages: Full refund 90+ days before
  • Last-minute bookings: Non-refundable within 14 days of booking

We strongly recommend travel insurance to protect your investment.
